# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 023
**Title:** Sweet Dreams, Bitter End
**Slug:** roommate-poison
**Category:** Fellow Travelers

## The Death (One Sentence)
A travel blogger slowly poisons her dormitory roommate over two weeks, adding small doses of antifreeze to shared food, driven by jealousy over social media success.

## Tagline
"She said the green tea was for detox. She wasn't wrong."

## Themes
1. Social media envy taken to its logical extreme - satirizes the toxic comparison culture among travelers
2. The intimacy of hostel living - how much we trust strangers with access to our food, belongings, bodies
3. The invisible violence of women - challenging assumptions about female aggression

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 20% (1-100)
- Horror: 55%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's felt jealous of another traveler's success, readers fascinated by female-perpetrated crime, people who've shared food with hostel strangers without a second thought.

## Comparison Titles
- "Gone Girl" meets "Emily in Paris" - with a body count

## Content Warnings
- Poisoning
- Premeditated murder
- Social media obsession
- Slow death
- Vomiting/illness descriptions
